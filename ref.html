How to Place Orders on Porto.com

To initiate your shopping journey, you can easily navigate to the Porto website through your desktop or mobile browser. If you prefer the convenience of a mobile app, you can download and install the Porto Shopping app from the App Store for iPhone users or the Play Store for Android users.

Steps to Place an Order:

  • Browse through our wide selection of products and select the items you wish to purchase.
  • Add the selected items to your "Shopping Cart" by clicking on the respective buttons.
  • Once you've completed your selection, review your shopping cart and click on "Proceed to Checkout."
  • You'll be prompted to sign in to your Porto account using your registered mobile number or email address. New customers can easily create an account by selecting the "I am a new customer" option.
  • Enter your delivery address or select a pickup store based on availability.
  • If you're purchasing a gift or wish to have your order gift-wrapped, you can select the gift-wrap option and include a personalized message.
  • Choose your preferred payment method, enter the required details, and proceed by clicking on "Continue."
  • Review your order details once more and click on "Place your Order" to complete the transaction. Note: Please ensure that you review your order carefully before placing it, as delivery speeds, dates, and delivery addresses cannot be modified once the order is confirmed.

    • How to Modify Order Quantities

    To adjust the quantity of items in your cart, simply navigate to your shopping cart and locate the quantity dropdown box next to each item. Select your desired quantity from the dropdown, and the order amount will be updated accordingly.

    Placing Bulk Orders:

    For bulk orders, Porto offers a specialized service through Porto Business. You can place bulk orders by visiting this link or by accessing the Bulk Ordering option through your Porto Business account.

    Payment Issues and Troubleshooting:

    If you encounter any issues while placing an order, it could be due to payment declines, item availability, technical glitches, or connectivity issues. Please refer to our Payment Help page for further assistance.

    Cash on Delivery:

    Porto.com offers Cash on Delivery as a payment option, allowing you to pay for your order upon delivery. For more information, please visit our Cash on Delivery page.

    Ordering Gifts

    You can choose to gift-wrap any item fulfilled by Porto during checkout. Simply select the gift-wrap option and include a personalized message for your recipient. Visit our Gift Order page to explore more options.

    Changing Payment Methods and Delivery Instructions

    You can manage your payment methods and update delivery instructions by accessing the respective sections in your Porto account. For detailed instructions, please refer to our Payment and Delivery Help page.

    Cancelling Orders and Returns:

    To cancel or return an order, visit the "Your Orders" section in your Porto account. Follow the provided instructions based on whether the order has been dispatched or not. For more information, please visit our Returns and Refunds page.

    Reordering Items

    Reordering previously purchased items is simple with Porto. Just visit the "Your Orders" section, click on the "Buy Again" tab, and proceed to add the desired items to your cart..

    Tracking Orders:

    You can track the status of your orders through Porto's delivery services, including Porto fulfilled, seller fulfilled, or delivery by third-party carriers. For detailed tracking instructions, please visit our Order Tracking page..

    For further assistance or inquiries, feel free to explore our Help Center or reach out to our customer support team.
